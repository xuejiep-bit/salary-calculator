How to Convert Salary to Hourly Wage

Converting your annual salary to an hourly rate is straightforward. The standard formula assumes a full-time work schedule in the United States: 40 hours per week for 52 weeks per year, which totals 2,080 working hours annually.

Salary to Hourly:

Hourly Rate = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year)

Example: $50,000 ÷ 2,080 = $24.04 per hour

For instance, if you earn a salary of $50,000 per year and work the standard 40-hour week for all 52 weeks, your hourly rate would be $24.04. If you take unpaid time off, your effective hourly rate increases because you are earning the same salary over fewer working hours.

How to Convert Hourly Wage to Annual Salary

To convert in the other direction — from an hourly wage to annual salary — simply reverse the formula. Multiply your hourly rate by the number of hours you work per week, then multiply by the number of weeks you work per year.

Hourly to Salary:

Annual Salary = Hourly Rate × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year

Example: $25 × 40 × 52 = $52,000 per year

This hourly to salary calculator is especially useful when comparing job offers presented in different pay formats. An offer of $28 per hour may sound less impressive than "$55,000 per year," but when you run the numbers, the hourly offer actually works out to $58,240 annually — a significantly higher wage.

Keep in mind that this calculator shows gross pay — your earnings before taxes and deductions. Your actual take-home pay will be lower depending on your federal and state tax rates, Social Security and Medicare contributions, health insurance premiums, and retirement contributions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many working hours are in a year?

A standard full-time work year in the United States is 2,080 hours (40 hours per week × 52 weeks).

How do I calculate my hourly rate from my salary?

Divide your annual salary by 2,080. For example, a $60,000 salary divided by 2,080 hours equals approximately $28.85 per hour.

What is $20 an hour annually?

At $20 per hour, working 40 hours a week for 52 weeks, you would earn $41,600 per year before taxes.

What salary is $30 an hour?

An hourly wage of $30, with a standard 40-hour workweek and 52 working weeks, equals an annual salary of $62,400.
